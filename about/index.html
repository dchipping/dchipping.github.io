About

Hello! I’m Daniel, currently at University College London (UCL) as a MSc Robotics & Computation student. I’m particularly interested in applied intelligence for real-world systems, from robotics to spatial AI.

Projects

  • Blockism (2022): Alongside coursemates, created a social virtual reality building game where players work together to build structures and score points before time runs out. The game is built within the Unity ecosystem with UCL’s Ubiq library for networking utilities.
  • Hybrid End-effector Concept (2020): As part of my Mechanical Engineering studies I created a hybridised fingered and suction grasper, which used novel system design to make use of fingered and suction grasping synergies. Received a few accolades including: CATIA Project of The Year Award, IMechE Project Award & UCL MechEng Best Project.
